## **Breaking down one of the most disruptive wealth creation events in history**


--------------------
View image: (https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/c4328a16-91c3-42dc-b2e3-719ca477df8a/superhumanteam_A_robot_who_looks_like_Mr_Monopoly_holding_two_6d843f5e-3fac-47df-821a-64e3953da0df_3.jpg?t=1775146578)
Caption: Made with Midjourney


--------------------
**Shattering records.** OpenAI **[closed](https://openai.com/index/accelerating-the-next-phase-ai/)** $122B in funding at an $852B valuation this week — the largest private raise ever that made OpenAI more valuable than Disney, Uber, and McDonald’s **[combined](https://x.com/content_grinder/status/2039507076784042431)**. The growth story is undeniable, but so is the fine print: OpenAI has yet to turn a profit.

**OpenAI’s numbers.** OpenAI now generates $2B in monthly revenue, has 900M weekly users, and says its ads pilot crossed $100M ARR in under six weeks. The strategic vision is to create a unified "superapp" merging ChatGPT, Codex, browsing, and agentic capabilities into a single system. But while the growth is rock solid, the $122B in fresh funding has some caveats.

**The fine print.** Amazon, Nvidia, and SoftBank accounted for 90% of the round, but that money comes with strings. $35B of Amazon's pledge is conditional on OpenAI going public or achieving AGI, and much of Nvidia's contribution is in GPU compute rather than cash. Since OpenAI spends heavily on both companies’ products, lots of the capital will [flow](https://builtin.com/articles/ai-circular-financing) right back to the investors who wrote the checks — a caveat that can make the round look bigger than it is.

**The elephant in the room?** Profit. OpenAI is projected to lose an estimated $44 billion before profitability arrives (optimistically) around 2029. Revenue has soared from $6B to $24B ARR in barely over a year, but will that growth be enough to outpace OpenAI’s burn rate? That’s the $852B question.
