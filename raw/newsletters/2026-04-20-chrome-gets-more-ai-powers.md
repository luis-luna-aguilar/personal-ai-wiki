**1. Anthropic teams up with Canva to launch Claude Design: **[Claude Design](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-design-anthropic-labs) lets users create exportable visuals like slides, one-pagers, or mock-ups by describing what they want and then refining the design through conversation, direct edits, or custom sliders. It’s powered by Opus 4.7 and rolling out to paid plans now. [See how it works](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t_LBECIQQqs) or learn [7 strategies](https://x.com/flomerboy/status/2045162321589252458) to generate the best results. 

**2. Chrome lets you use Search side-by-side with AI Mode: **When using AI Mode in Chrome, clicking a link will now [open](https://blog.google/products-and-platforms/products/search/ai-mode-chrome/) the new page directly next to your existing page, instead of in a new tab. The Search giant says this should reduce the amount of “tab hopping” required when searching the web. [See how it works.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HuZUmhGcg-I)

**3. OpenAI loses three executives as it winds down "side projects":** Sora head Bill Peebles, OpenAI for Science lead Kevin Weil, and enterprise CTO Srinivas Narayanan all [announced](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/17/kevin-weil-and-bill-peebles-exit-openai-as-company-continues-to-shed-side-quests/) their departures Friday. The triple exit follows OpenAI's decision to deprioritize other projects and consolidate around enterprise AI, Codex, and building a super-app.

## Funnel Design and Optimization

```
Prompt: Act as a growth marketing expert with deep experience in performance marketing, user psychology, and funnel optimization. Design a complete, end-to-end marketing funnel for the following offer: [describe offer], tailored specifically for the target audience: [describe audience, including demographics, behaviours, and pain points]. Break the funnel into the four key stages: awareness, consideration, conversion, and retention. For each stage, recommend specific content types (e.g., ads, landing pages, emails, social posts), distribution channels (e.g., Meta, Google, LinkedIn, organic, partnerships), messaging angles (emotional hooks, value propositions, objections to address), and clear calls to action. Go deeper by explaining why each recommendation fits that stage of the funnel, how it aligns with user intent and psychology, and how it moves the user to the next step. Include suggestions for lead magnets, nurture sequences, retargeting strategies, and conversion optimization tactics. Also outline key metrics to track at each stage (e.g., CTR, CAC, conversion rate, LTV) and potential bottlenecks with ideas to improve performance. Ensure the funnel is cohesive, scalable, and optimized for both short-term conversions and long-term retention.
```
