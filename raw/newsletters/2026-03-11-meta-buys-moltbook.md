**1. Google supercharges Docs, Sheets, Slides, and Drive with Gemini-powered creation and cross-app intelligence:** Gemini is now [embedded](https://blog.google/products-and-platforms/products/workspace/gemini-workspace-updates-march-2026/) directly into Docs, Sheets, Slides, and Drive — each now capable of pulling context from your files, emails, and the web to generate personalized content. Docs can [write first drafts](https://blog.google/products-and-platforms/products/workspace/gemini-workspace-updates-march-2026/#:~:text=Go%20from%20a%20blank%20doc%20to%20a%20final%20draft) that mirror your writing style, Sheets can [draft spreadsheets](https://blog.google/products-and-platforms/products/workspace/gemini-workspace-updates-march-2026/#:~:text=Get%20started%20quickly%20with%20spreadsheets%20that%20build%20themselves) from scratch and auto-populate tables with real-time info from Google Search, and Slides can [generate](https://blog.google/products-and-platforms/products/workspace/gemini-workspace-updates-march-2026/#:~:text=Go%20from%20concept%20to%20presentation%2Dready%20in%20Slides) fully editable, theme-matched slides on demand. Plus, Drive now answers complex natural-language questions across your documents, emails, calendar, and the web. All features are rolling out now in beta to Google AI Ultra and Pro subscribers. [Watch the new features in action.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=o5Ur1pm4oDA)

**2. Meta Platforms buys Moltbook, the AI agent social network:** Meta acquired [**Moltbook**](https://www.moltbook.com/), the Reddit-like social media platform where AI agents powered by [**OpenClaw**](https://openclaw.ai/) can interact with one another — posting, commenting, and organizing autonomously. Moltbook co-founders Matt Schlicht and Ben Parr are [joining](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/10/meta-acquired-moltbook-the-ai-agent-social-network-that-went-viral-because-of-fake-posts/) Meta Superintelligence Labs as part of the deal, though full terms were not disclosed. The acquisition comes just weeks after OpenClaw creator Peter Steinberger joined OpenAI in a similar acquihire, signaling that the major AI labs are rapidly snapping up the talent behind the emerging agent infrastructure layer.

**3. Yann LeCun officially unveils AMI Labs, a world model startup that just raised $1.03 billion**: Turing Prize winner Yann LeCun formally [announced](https://x.com/amilabs/status/2031234832454324639) Advanced Machine Intelligence (AMI) Labs, his new startup building world models — or AI systems that learn from the real world rather than text. The company has raised $1.03B at a $3.5B pre-money valuation with a leadership team that includes CEO Alexandre LeBrun (former Meta AI/Nabla founder), COO Laurent Solly (Meta’s former VP for Europe), and chief science officer Saining Xie. AMI Labs says it will publish research openly and make much of its code open source. Learn more [here](https://amilabs.xyz/).

## **How to create notes in your own handwriting using Gemini**


--------------------
View image: (https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/83a140f8-7661-4395-aa42-a8b97d4001e3/Image_from_Squoosh__48_.jpg?t=1773219873)
Caption: Image source: Preda2005


--------------------
* Go to [NanoBanana](https://gemini.google.com/) and sign up.

* Upload a small sample of your handwriting (just a few lines on paper).

* Enter a prompt like this:

_Sample Prompt: “Analyze the uploaded handwriting sample and match its style exactly (letter shapes, spacing, slant, and stroke flow). Write the following text in the same handwriting style: [sample text]”_

* Click generate.

* Within seconds, Claude recreates your handwriting and writes the content directly in that style.

* It looks like you personally wrote it.
