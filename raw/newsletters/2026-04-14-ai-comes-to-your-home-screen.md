## **How to write and edit Word docs faster with Claude**


--------------------
View image: (https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/57e687e6-3a9b-4365-8788-dc566acd4264/Figma_Group_1000005183.jpg?t=1776071300)
Caption: 


--------------------
* Install [Claude for Word](https://claude.com/claude-for-word), open Microsoft Word and sign in with your Team or Enterprise account that has Claude enabled

* Open any document you want to work on

* Click the Claude sidebar (usually on the right side of Word) to launch it

* Highlight the text you want help with (or keep the document unselected for full-document edits)

* Type a prompt like “rewrite this professionally” or “summarize this section”

* Review Claude’s suggestions directly in the document

* Ask follow-ups in the sidebar to refine tone, length, or clarity

* Repeat until your document is polished
