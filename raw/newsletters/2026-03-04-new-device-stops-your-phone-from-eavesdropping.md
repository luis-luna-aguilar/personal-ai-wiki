**1. Alibaba, Google, and OpenAI all drop new models targeting speed, size, and tone:** Alibaba kicked off the wave with the [Qwen 3.5 Small](https://x.com/Alibaba_Qwen/status/2028460046510965160) series, a family of open-source models built to run [AI directly on devices](https://x.com/adrgrondin/status/2028568689709084919) — even without an internet connection. We also got [Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/models-and-research/gemini-models/gemini-3-1-flash-lite/?utm_source=x&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=&utm_content=), the cheapest and fastest Gemini 3 series model yet, capable of outperforming 2.5 Flash with faster performance at a cheaper price. Finally, OpenAI released [GPT-5.3 Instant,](https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-3-instant/) a model it describes as "more accurate, less cringe” that delivers answers without unnecessary preambles. All three are rolling out now.

**2. Spectre I is a new portable device that stops your phone, laptop, and smart speakers from listening to your conversations:** [Deveillance](https://www.deveillance.com/), a new startup focused on consumer privacy, just launched Spectre I — a portable device that emits signals that overlay with your speech and cloak it from all microphones within a 2-meter radius to help ensure privacy. The device’s signals are inaudible to humans and run completely on-device with no cloud processing. Spectre I is available for pre-order now with shipping expected in August 2026. The release video has already racked up 1M views. [Watch it in action here.](https://x.com/aidaxbaradari/status/2028864606568067491)

**3. Chronicle turns raw notes into polished, on-brand presentations:** Chronicle is an AI-powered storytelling startup that recently released Muse, an agent that handles research, copywriting, layout, and iteration to [create beautiful presentations](https://chroniclehq.com/ai-presentation-maker). The platform includes 200+ templates, lets you embed common tools directly into slides, and exports to PPT, PDF, or a shareable web link with built-in viewer analytics. See how it works [here](https://x.com/tejgw/status/2028674354062503985).

## **Venture funding hit $189B in February — up 780% from last year**


--------------------
View image: (https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/6d32a812-7a08-449f-a1f5-9e29c4805798/superhumanteam_One_robot_sitting_on_a_tall_pile_of_money_--ar_07a60885-d6c8-45a9-afaa-47f2451b50f7_2.jpg?t=1772586367)
Caption: 


--------------------
**Setting records.** Global venture capital funding [hit](https://news.crunchbase.com/venture/record-setting-global-funding-february-2026-openai-anthropic/) $189B in February, the largest month ever recorded — and a 780% increase over February 2025. It was an unprecedented month that [this chart](https://news.crunchbase.com/venture/record-setting-global-funding-february-2026-openai-anthropic/#:~:text=AI%2Drelated%20startups%20raised%20%24171%20billion%20in%20February%2C%20accounting%20for%2090%25%20of%20global%20venture%20funding.%20Other%20sectors%20that%20stood%20out%20include%20hardware%2Drelated%20startups%20dominated%20by%20autonomous%2Dvehicle%20technology%2C%20semiconductors%2C%20robotics%20and%20networking%20products.) really puts into perspective. But the real story isn’t just the record funding. It’s where that money went.

**Consolidated capital.** Just three companies raked in 83% of all funding last month: OpenAI, Anthropic, and Waymo. OpenAI's latest round (a $110B round backed by SoftBank, Nvidia, and Amazon) would’ve been enough to set the monthly funding record by itself. We’ve grown accustomed to AI firms raising billions — but OpenAI is in a league of its own.

**The public/private split**. The euphoria in the private markets is in stark contrast to the stock market — which saw software stocks shed [roughly](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/us-software-stocks-stabilize-bruising-121536263.html) $1T in early February after a wave of Anthropic product releases spooked investors. The bleeding only [**slowed**](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/24/software-stocks-anthropic-ai.html) when Anthropic announced several partnerships and eased concerns of AI replacing software entirely.  

**Both markets will collide soon.** OpenAI and Anthropic are reportedly [exploring](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/openai-eyes-biggest-us-ipo-162525000.html) IPOs this year, either of which would rank among the largest offerings in history. But going public also means opening their books for the first time — and publicly justifying their sky-high valuations.

## Preparing for a key prospect meeting



```
Prompt: 
Goal: Be fully prepared to discuss a prospect's needs and demonstrate how your solution can help.
Find:
"Find all CRM records, past email correspondence, and meeting notes for prospect [Prospect Company Name]."
"Locate recent news articles or press releases about [Prospect Company Name] and their industry." (Requires web access/specific data feeds)
"Show me our product specification sheets and relevant case studies for solutions applicable to the [Prospect's Industry]."
Understand:
"Summarize [Prospect Company Name]'s key business challenges and stated needs based on our past interactions."
"Identify the key decision-makers and influencers at [Prospect Company Name] involved in this potential deal."
"Which of [your company/division name] product features directly address [Prospect Company Name]'s identified pain points?"
Act:
"Generate a list of 5 tailored talking points and 5 discovery questions for my upcoming meeting with [Prospect Company Name], focusing on how our solutions address their specific challenges in [Prospect's Industry]."
“You are a Sales Executive. Draft a personalized follow-up email to send after the meeting with [Prospect Company Name]. Summarize key discussion points and outline clear next steps. Keep it concise and professional."
(Requires agent/action/integration): "Log a pre-meeting preparation note in the [your CRM app] for [Prospect Company Name] outlining my key objectives for the upcoming call on [Date]."
```
