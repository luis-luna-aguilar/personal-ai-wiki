##### **FROM THE FRONTIER**

## **Can blasting weeds with lasers really solve farming’s herbicide problem? **


--------------------
View image: (https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/8a4905c0-31a4-4c89-88d9-3f17bf30934c/superhumanteam_A_robot_shooting_a_laser_gun_at_weeds_in_a_far_ea28d582-0844-488f-90de-3aef6a3d7df8_1.jpg?t=1772729498)
Caption: Made with Midjourney


--------------------
**Creating chemical-free fields.** For decades, herbicides have been farming's necessary evil: crucial for removing weeds and maximizing yields, but harmful for ecosystems, waterways, and the food on our plates. One AI-powered machine offers a solution: swapping herbicide spray for lasers.

**Meet the LaserWeeder G2.** Carbon Robotics’ LaserWeeder G2 (which went [viral](https://x.com/rainmaker1973/status/2029207061876060468) this week) is a tractor-pulled machine armed with cameras, Nvidia GPUs, and high-powered lasers. It scans the field in front of it, instantly identifies weeds, and destroys them with alarming accuracy: 450,000 weeds per hour, sub-millimeter precision, and a 99% success rate. The best part: Weeds can't build resistance to lasers, like they do with herbicides.

**Other AI breakthroughs in farming.** Carbon Robotics isn't the only company rethinking weed control. Stout Industrial's [Smart Cultivator](https://www.stout.ai/) uses AI-enhanced mechanical blades to physically cut weeds from the ground, removing the need for herbicides. Similarly, John Deere's [**See & Spray**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OqIRBgDKtAQ) system uses mounted cameras and AI to detect weeds in real time and only spray where needed, cutting usage dramatically.

**The future of farming.** The AI industry isn’t known for being green, with its dramatic energy demands, water-guzzling data centers, and high carbon emissions. But use cases like laser-powered weed removal are a welcome counterpoint. If AI can help protect our ecosystems and food supply from harmful chemicals, the future starts to look a bit more favorable.

##### **PROMPT STATION**

## Learn Any Technical/Coding Topic

```
Prompt: You are an expert coding tutor who excels at breaking down complex technical concepts for learners at any level.

I want to learn about: [enter topic]
Teach me using the following structure:
---
LAYER 1 — Explain Like I'm 5  
Explain this concept using a simple, fun real-world analogy, a 5-year-old 
would understand. No technical terms. Just pure intuition building.
---
LAYER 2 — The Real Explanation  
Now explain the concept properly. Cover:
- What it is  
- Why it exists / what problem it solves  
- How it works at a fundamental level  
- A simple code example if applicable (with brief inline comments)  
Keep explanations concise but not oversimplified.
---
LAYER 3 — Now I Get It (Key Takeaways)  
Summarise the concept in 2-3 crisp bullet points
---
MISCONCEPTION ALERT  
Call out 1–2 common mistakes or wrong assumptions developers make.Call out 1-2 of the most common mistakes or wrong assumptions developers make about this topic. Be direct and specific.
---
OPTIONAL — Further Exploration  
Suggest 2–3 related subtopics to study next.
---
Tone: friendly, clear, practical.  
Avoid jargon in Layer 1. Be technically precise in Layer 2. Avoid filler sentences.
```
