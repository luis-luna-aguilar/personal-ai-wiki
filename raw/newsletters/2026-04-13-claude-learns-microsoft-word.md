## **HumanX was last week — and AI leaders couldn’t stop talking about these 3 topics**


--------------------
View image: (https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/42f7db2a-2167-4889-a0ac-1fe09f472c51/superhumanteam_Three_robots_sitting_on_stage_talking_to_busin_a409d3d2-f2d8-4dc3-9173-57bdf5d44c53_2.jpg?t=1776029091)
Caption: Made with Midjourney


--------------------
**The conference.** 6,700 executives, founders, and investors flocked to San Francisco last week for [HumanX](https://www.humanx.co/): a conference that’s quickly cementing itself as a premier event in the AI space. Three key topics dominated the conversation.

**Claude mania.** This year’s fan favorite was unmistakably Anthropic and Claude Code. It was a stark reversal from the debut conference, with venture capitalist Roseanne Wincek [saying](https://www.businessinsider.com/the-consensus-was-openai-is-cooked-and-anthropic-is-cooking-2026-4#:~:text=%22In%20Vegas%20last%20year%2C%20it%20felt%20like%20OpenAI%20was%20the%20clear%20winner%2C%20and%20now%20it%20seems%20like%20Anthropic%20is%20miles%20ahead%2C%22%20said%20Roseanne%20Winsek%20of%20Renegade%20Partners.%20%22The%20Anthropic%20product%20is%20so%20good.%22), “Last year OpenAI looked like the clear winner; this year Anthropic feels miles ahead.” However, one investor [pointed out](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/11/vibe-check-from-ai-industry-humanx-anthropic-is-talk-of-the-town.html#:~:text=One%20investor%20cautioned%20that%20while%20Anthropic%20has%20been%20consistent%20and%20managed%20to%20identify%20a%20sticky%20AI%20use%20case%2C%20the%20industry%20is%20still%20young%2C%20and%20momentum%20could%20easily%20swing%20in%20another%20direction.%C2%A0) that the momentum can swing back just as quickly — a pattern the AI industry has seen play out dozens of times already.

**The AI adoption bottleneck.** Many speakers mirrored another sentiment: implementing AI is easy, but transforming a company or department around it is much harder. One winning formula? Rethinking workflows from scratch to be AI-forward, instead of just speeding up existing processes.

**China's squeeze.** The conference’s closing takeaway: China is increasingly putting pressure on the US by undercutting American labs on price with competitive open-weight models, while also attracting top Chinese researchers working in the US back home.
