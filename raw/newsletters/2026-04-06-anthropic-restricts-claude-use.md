##### **FROM THE FRONTIER**

## **Sam Altman was right. The first one-person $1 Billion companies are coming.**


--------------------
View image: (https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/c5c113de-91ad-473d-9d16-ad69e5e95b0d/superhumanteam_A_businessman_wearing_a_bionic_robot_suit_--ar_2cc34704-f14d-448d-b501-717d42d3b8f9_0.jpg?t=1775234703)
Caption: Made with Midjourney


--------------------
**The bold prediction. **In 2024, Sam Altman predicted that AI would enable a single person to scale a company to $1B in revenue — and a 41-year-old from LA already has. Matthew Gallagher launched [Medvi](https://home.medvi.org/) a year and a half ago with $20,000 and a dozen AI tools. It’s on track to reach $1.8 billion in sales this year.

**The business model. **Medvi is a telehealth company specializing in compounded semaglutide, the main ingredient in Ozempic. It controls the customer relationship (ad funnel, website, customer service) but offloads the doctors, prescriptions, and medication to OpenLoop and CareValidate, regulated white-label telehealth platforms.

**How he did it. **Gallagher [leans on](https://www.inc.com/leila-sheridan/the-no-employee-billion-dollar-startup-how-ai-is-changing-the-face-of-solopreneurship/91326517) AI for nearly everything at Medvi: ChatGPT, Claude, and Grok write the code, Midjourney and Runway generate ads, and ElevenLabs handles customer service. The only other employee? His younger brother, Elliot. That lean setup translates to a 16.2% net margin — nearly triple the 5.5% that competitor Hims & Hers posts with thousands of employees on payroll.

**The red flags. **The same AI stack fueling Medvi's growth is also raising eyebrows — including [allegations](https://futurism.com/medvi-ai-ozempic) of hundreds of fake doctor accounts running Facebook ads on the company's behalf. Gallagher’s story is proof that AI is a force multiplier: capable of powering both growth and deception at the same scale.
