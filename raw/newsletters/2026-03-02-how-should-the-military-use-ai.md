**1. Anthropic pushes back on Pentagon and gets blacklisted by the White House, while OpenAI lands contract: **Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei [personally refused](https://www.anthropic.com/news/statement-department-of-war?utm_source=superhuman&utm_medium=newsletter&utm_campaign=how-should-the-military-use-ai) to give the military unrestricted access to Claude, drawing two red lines for AI usage: no mass surveillance of Americans and no fully autonomous weapons. In response, the government designated Anthropic a supply chain risk to national security — a label previously reserved for foreign adversaries. Anthropic says it will challenge the designation in court. Shortly after, OpenAI [struck](https://x.com/sama/status/2027578652477821175) its own [classified-network deal](https://openai.com/index/our-agreement-with-the-department-of-war/) with the Pentagon that had the same red lines Anthropic had been fighting for. 90+ OpenAI employees and 600+ Google employees have signed an [**open letter**](https://notdivided.org/) urging their companies to back Anthropic's stance. Read more [here](https://www.npr.org/2026/02/27/nx-s1-5729118/trump-anthropic-pentagon-openai-ai-weapons-ban).

**2. Read AI launches Ada, an email-based "digital twin" that handles your calendar and inbox while you sleep**: [Ada](https://www.read.ai/digital-twin) is a new AI assistant from [Read AI](https://www.read.ai/) that lives entirely in your email — managing your schedule, answering questions from your company's knowledge base and past meetings, and drafting replies. Just CC Ada on a scheduling thread and it replies with your availability, negotiates alternate time slots, or books the meeting (without revealing what's already on your calendar). Read AI now has over 5 million monthly active users with 50,000 new signups per day. To try it, email [**ada@read.ai**](mailto:ada@read.ai) with "Get me started."

**3. Anthropic adds auto memory to Claude Code:** [Auto-memory](https://x.com/trq212/status/2027109375765356723) is a new feature for [Claude Code](https://claude.com/product/claude-code) that lets it automatically save and recall context across sessions — including build commands, debugging patterns, architecture decisions, and code style preferences — without developers having to write anything down. It stores notes in a local MEMORY.md file per project (with the first 200 lines loaded into every new session), creates topic-specific files, and decides on its own what's worth remembering. The feature is on by default and can be toggled via the /memory command.

## **Block just fired 4,000 people due to an internal AI tool — and its stock soared**


--------------------
View image: (https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/b696bfb8-4ecf-48cb-814c-f09fd1170e2c/superhumanteam_A_company_announcing_mass_layoffs_--ar_169_--s_572afb75-1d54-4b60-aa31-d68f790d609d_0.jpg?t=1772212919)
Caption: Made with Midjourney


--------------------
**Block cuts its workforce. **On Thursday, Block cut roughly half its employees due to AI while posting its best quarter in company history. But this wasn’t another corporate restructuring vaguely attributed to AI. CEO Jack Dorsey documented his reasoning in a [post](https://x.com/jack/status/2027129697092731343) that’s racked up 62M views — and it offers a glimpse into what the future might hold.

**The receipts.** Block’s business isn’t struggling. It’s thriving. The company just posted 24% quarterly gross profit growth, raised guidance for next year, and can afford [generous](https://techcrunch.com/2026/02/26/jack-dorsey-block-layoffs-4000-halved-employees-your-company-is-next/#:~:text=As%20for%20severance,policies%2C%20he%20added.) severance packages. So why the layoffs? Dorsey attributed the decision to an internal AI agent called Goose, which has boosted code output per engineer by 40% and “enabled a new way of working.” 

**The stock market’s verdict. **With these layoffs,** **Block nearly doubled its revenue per employee from roughly $2.2M to $4.2M — and the stock soared 15+%. The message to every leadership team with stock-based compensation packages is now clear: Replacing headcount with AI gets rewarded.

**But this is still a gamble. **Block is betting that AI has already crossed the threshold from "promising technology" to "load-bearing infrastructure." Jack Dorsey himself even admitted this decision was a bit preemptive. The real test will come in two quarters, when the smaller organization has to outperform last year’s numbers.

## **How to optimize your LinkedIn profile with Gemini**


--------------------
View image: (https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/97c1af19-8e99-4bab-884e-1f9593e22178/Figma_Group_1000005117__2_.jpg?t=1772384650)

--------------------
* Go to your LinkedIn profile → Click More → Select ‘Save to PDF’.

* Open [Google Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/) Pro and enable ‘Canvas ‘ and ‘Thinking’.

* Upload your profile PDF and enter the prompt

_Sample Prompt: [upload profile] Before rebuilding and optimizing my LinkedIn profile, ask me a few clarifying questions. Request my primary goal (sales calls, leads, job offers, newsletter growth, authority positioning, etc.). Ask for my target audience (be specific). Ask for my main offer (what I want people to buy, book, or join). Ask for up to two Featured links (URL + description). Ask for key social proof to highlight (audience size, revenue, brands, media mentions, etc.). Once you have this, rewrite my full LinkedIn profile including headline, About section, and experience._

* Answer the questions provided and Gemini rewrites your entire profile including new headline, about section, and experience.

* Select sections and click Extend to make edits.

* Copy the optimized sections back into LinkedIn.

That’s how you optimize your LinkedIn profile professionally.

## Personal Branding Architect

```
Prompt: 
You are a world-class Personal Branding Architect, skilled at helping individuals uncover, craft, and amplify their authentic professional identity to stand out meaningfully across digital and live platforms.



The user seeks to build a clear, magnetic, and authentic personal brand for LinkedIn profiles, portfolios, public speaking gigs, and/or personal websites.



Guide the user through a transformative three-phase journey:

**Phase 1: Discovery**
- Lead introspective exploration into passions, values, standout achievements, key life experiences, unique skills, and mission.
- Clarify their ideal audience and intended impact.
- Identify moments that shaped their professional philosophy.

**Phase 2: Articulation**
- Help define an authentic voice: ask about preferred tone (e.g., inspiring, relatable, authoritative) and emotional resonance.
- Uncover key phrases, metaphors, and self-descriptive adjectives.
- Co-create:
- A bold Personal Brand Statement (1–2 sentences)
- A short bio (75–100 words) adaptable for LinkedIn or introductions
- 2–3 signature story themes for content and speaking
- 3 key content pillars to build thought leadership

**Phase 3: Amplification**
- Refine language to be powerful, concise, and memorable.
- Offer suggestions for consistency across digital and live platforms.
- Create a Personal Brand Manifesto: a motivating paragraph capturing the user’s essence, mission, and evolving vision.

Source: Marino25
```
