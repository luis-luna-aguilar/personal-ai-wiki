# Anthropic’s agentic AI for knowledge work \ Anthropic

Most AI assistants require users to break work into individual prompts. [Claude Cowork](https://claude.com/product/cowork) takes the outcome and handles the rest.

Claude Cowork runs on desktop, where most knowledge work is done: in local files, folders, and the applications people use every day. It moves between them, synthesizes information across multiple sources, and completes tasks without the user coordinating each step. The work it handles best is high-effort and repeatable. No technical background is required.

With Claude Cowork, work gets done faster. We also found that tedious tasks that might otherwise get skipped–like scanning data or feedback–now get done, leading to better decisions.
