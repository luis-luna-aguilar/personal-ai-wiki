Add ChatGPT for Excel⁠ from Home → Add-ins, then search for ChatGPT. You should then see ChatGPT in the ribbon above your workbook. Open it and sign in with the OpenAI account that has your ChatGPT Plus, Pro, Business, or Enterprise plan.

Frequently asked questions

How is data handled for ChatGPT users?

By default, data shared with ChatGPT isn’t used to improve our models for ChatGPT Business⁠, ChatGPT Enterprise⁠, ChatGPT Edu⁠, and ChatGPT for Teachers⁠.

Can Enterprise administrators control access to ChatGPT for Excel?

For ChatGPT Enterprise workspaces, this feature is disabled by default. Administrators can enable it and manage who can use it through role-based access controls in their workspace settings⁠.

What does beta mean?

This is an early release. Features and performance may change as we learn from feedback, and results may be incomplete or incorrect. Always review formulas, calculations, and summaries before you share or rely on them.

How does ChatGPT for Excel connect to my ChatGPT account?

The ChatGPT for Excel add-in operates separately from your ChatGPT chat history. Conversations and data in Excel aren’t shared with your ChatGPT chats, and activity doesn’t sync between experiences at this time.

Is this available worldwide?

Yes. During beta, ChatGPT for Excel is available globally for ChatGPT Business, Enterprise, Edu, Teachers, and K-12 users, and for ChatGPT Pro and Plus users.

What are the limitations or risks?

Some advanced Excel capabilities aren’t supported yet, including Office Scripts, Power Query, and Pivot/Data Model, data validation, and the named ranges manager, slicers, timelines, external connection administration, advanced charting breadth (beyond the current supported set), and macro/Visual Basic for Applications (VBA) automation. For very large workbooks, some datasets may not fit within the context window, which can lead to partial results. 

ChatGPT isn’t a financial or accounting advisor and isn’t a substitute for professional judgment. Because it can edit your workbook, it may accidentally change or delete data if a request is unclear, so be specific about what to update and double-check what changed so you can revert back if needed.

What file types can I upload?

You can upload:

Images: JPEG/JPG, PNG, GIF

Documents: PDF, Word (.docx), RTF, OpenDocument text (.odt), Apple Pages/Keynote

Presentations: PowerPoint (.pptx)

Spreadsheets: Excel, CSV/TSV/IIF

Code & text: Plain text, Markdown, HTML, JSON, TOML, YAML, CSS, and common code types

Other text formats: Email/EML, ICS calendar, VCF contacts, subtitle files (.srt, .vtt)


Key limits:

Up to 20 attachments per message

25 MB max per file

Can ChatGPT for Excel and Google Sheets connect to my apps?

Yes. ChatGPT for Excel and Google Sheets can connect to apps⁠. Apps from your ChatGPT account can be used in ChatGPT for Excel and Google Sheets, subject to your plan, workspace admin settings, app availability, user permissions, and data-source entitlements.
