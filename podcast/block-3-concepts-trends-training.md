# Block 3 — Concepts, Trends & Enablement

**Target length:** ~40 minutes.

**Objective for this episode:** Keep the listener oriented to where the field is heading and how to bring a team along for the ride. After listening, they should recognize the load-bearing ideas, the trends worth watching, and the current playbooks for AI rollout inside a company.

**Framing for the podcast generator:**
- Three movements: concepts → trends → training. Signal each transition explicitly.
- Concepts are *ideas*, not products. Explain each in plain language before using jargon. If the source uses an unexplained technical term, define it in the same breath.
- Trends are *things being watched*. Say what's happening, what's driving it, and what would falsify the trend.
- Training pages are *operational*. Read the playbooks, patterns, and failure modes — these are the parts a listener can act on Monday morning.
- Connect forward: when a concept shows up that matches a trend or a training pattern, name the link.
- Strip filler: no recaps, no "in today's episode", no outros. Essay-style but tight.

**What follows:** the full content of every concept, trend, and training page, concatenated. Do not invent patterns, statistics, or examples that aren't below.
