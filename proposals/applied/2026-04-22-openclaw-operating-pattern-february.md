# OpenClaw operating pattern in late February

This source adds a practical onboarding layer to the OpenClaw story. The key shift is from novelty demos toward repeatable first-agent setup: persistent agents in messaging tools, bounded responsibilities, and routine usage patterns that can later scale into broader organizational adoption.

## Influenced pages

- [[training/company-wide-ai-enablement]] — adds first-agent onboarding as a practical adoption pattern
- [[trends/agents-reshape-organizations]] — reinforces early-March movement from demos to operational usage

## Key claims extracted

- Personal agents are easier to adopt when they start with one bounded role
- Messaging-native persistence lowers activation energy compared with separate AI apps
- The pattern is becoming operational and teachable, not only experimental
