```markdown
# Is harness engineering real?

This source is useful because it states the debate plainly. One camp argues frontier reasoning models increasingly make complex scaffolding unnecessary; the other argues the real product advantage still comes from harness quality: context, tools, evaluation, loop design, and workflow packaging. The source does not settle the debate, but it makes the competitive surface visible.

## Influenced pages

- [[concepts/harness]] — strengthens the conceptual framing of harness engineering as a first-class systems question

## Key claims extracted

- A harness is the orchestration layer around the model rather than the model itself
- Frontier labs often argue stronger reasoning models should reduce the need for scaffolding
- Harness-heavy practitioners argue context, tools, and evaluation still drive real-world performance differences
- The debate is becoming central enough to function as its own design question across agent systems
```
