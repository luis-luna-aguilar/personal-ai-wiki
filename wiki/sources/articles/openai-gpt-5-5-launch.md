Official launch announcement for GPT-5.5. The strongest signals are broad benchmark gains over GPT-5.4 in coding, knowledge work, math, cybersecurity, and scientific-use evaluations, combined with concrete product integration into Codex and ChatGPT.

## Key claims extracted

- GPT-5.5 released April 23, 2026 and is described as OpenAI's smartest and most intuitive model yet
- GPT-5.5 leads GPT-5.4 and Claude Opus 4.7 on Terminal-Bench 2.0, GDPval, ARC-AGI-2, and CyberGym among the published comparison set
- Claude Opus 4.7 still leads GPT-5.5 on SWE-Bench Pro, MCP Atlas, and FinanceAgent in OpenAI's own comparison table
- GPT-5.5 powers Codex with a 400K context window; OpenAI also references a 1M-context API offering
- OpenAI reports 85%+ weekly internal Codex usage and says GPT-5.5 solves Codex tasks with fewer tokens than GPT-5.4
- The launch materials frame GPT-5.5 as materially stronger for early scientific research and cyber workflows, not only for coding/chat use
