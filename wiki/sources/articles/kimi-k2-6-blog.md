# Kimi K2.6 — Advancing Open-Source Coding

Moonshot AI's official launch post for Kimi K2.6. Covers the model architecture, benchmark claims, long-horizon coding demos, frontend design comparisons, Agent Swarm scaling, Claw Groups multi-agent coordination, and enterprise beta feedback.

## Key claims extracted

- 1T-parameter MoE, 32B active, 384 experts, 256K context, native multimodal support, INT4 quantization
- Claimed open-source SOTA on HLE w/ tools 54.0, SWE-Bench Pro 58.6, SWE-bench Multilingual 76.7, and BrowseComp 83.2
- Claimed scaling to 300 sub-agents and 4,000 coordinated steps at once, versus 100 sub-agents / 1,500 steps for K2.5
- Claw Groups is presented as a shared operational space where different agents, devices, and models can collaborate
- Moonshot reports a 5-day autonomous infrastructure-agent run from its RL infra team
