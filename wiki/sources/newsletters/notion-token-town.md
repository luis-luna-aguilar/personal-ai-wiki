# Notion's Token Town / software factory discussion

Latent Space's Notion interview is useful less as product news than as a window into orchestration discipline. The strongest signals are repeated harness rebuilds, internal-first prototypes, progressive tool disclosure, evals that separate launch readiness from frontier headroom, and an engineering culture willing to throw away scaffolding as models and constraints change.
