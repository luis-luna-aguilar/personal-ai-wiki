# GPT-5.4

This source cluster treats GPT-5.4 as OpenAI's strongest general-purpose model in early March 2026, especially for reasoning, browsing, coding, and agentic tasks. It also adds important caveats: Claude still appears stronger on writing/design-heavy work, GPT-5.3 Codex may still be preferable for some coding-style tasks, and the 1M-context story looks weaker in practice than in headline form.

## Key claims extracted

- GPT-5.4 is framed as the strongest general-purpose OpenAI model in the captured source cycle
- It performs well on reasoning, browsing, coding, and agent workflows
- The 1M-context variant exists but appears to degrade materially past roughly 256K tokens
- Claude remains stronger on writing style, personality, and design-sensitive work
