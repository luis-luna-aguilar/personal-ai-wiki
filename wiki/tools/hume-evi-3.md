# Hume EVI 3

Hume EVI 3 is a speech-to-speech voice model centered on emotionally aware conversation. It matters less as generic TTS and more as a conversational voice interface with strong affect and expression control.

## Current status (as of 2026-04-22)

- Positioned around empathic and emotionally expressive conversation
- More relevant to live voice interaction than to offline narration
- Strong fit for the "voice as agent interface" trend
