# OpenAI Deep Research

OpenAI's productized deep research agent. Available via ChatGPT and the OpenAI API. Runs a multi-step research loop — planning, web browsing, source synthesis — to produce long-form cited research outputs rather than single-pass answers.

## Current status (as of 2026-04-22)

- Available in ChatGPT (Pro and above) and via OpenAI API
- Product shape: planning phase → iterative web browsing → synthesis → cited long-form report
- Part of the same product tier as other long-horizon agent capabilities

## Why it matters

Along with Gemini Deep Research, this establishes long-horizon research agents as a standard product category rather than a research demo. See [Deep Research (concept)](../concepts/deep-research.md) for the category concept and [Gemini Deep Research](gemini-deep-research.md) for Google's implementation with published benchmark scores.
