# Hebbia

Hebbia is an AI knowledge-work platform with especially strong relevance in finance. In this wiki it belongs under finance AI because the durable signal is document-heavy financial analysis and modeling work, not generic chat assistance.

## Current status (as of 2026-04-22)

- Strong fit for finance workflows involving large document sets
- Also adjacent to legal and research-heavy work, but finance looks like the clearest primary home
