# LandingAI Agentic Document Extraction

LandingAI's Agentic Document Extraction is a document-intelligence system built for visually complex documents. The important distinction is the product's emphasis on preserving structural context across tables, forms, and segmented document layouts.

## Current status (as of 2026-04-22)

- Explicitly marketed as agentic document extraction rather than plain OCR
- Stronger on structured document reasoning than simple text recovery
- Relevant for finance and other document-heavy operations
