# OpenEvidence

OpenEvidence is a physician-oriented medical information tool for evidence lookup and clinical questions. It is one of the clearest "medical knowledge layer" products in the workbook.

## Current status (as of 2026-04-22)

- Built around source-backed medical answers for clinicians
- Better framed as a healthcare knowledge tool than as a general-purpose chatbot
- Strong fit for keeping healthcare AI separate from generic assistants
