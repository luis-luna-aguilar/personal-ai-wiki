# Claude Design

Anthropic's research-preview artifact-generation surface for prototypes, slides, and one-pagers. It turns natural-language prompting into presentation and design outputs, positioning Claude less as a chat box and more as a tool for shipping polished business artifacts.

## Current status (as of 2026-04-21)

- Research-preview surface for generating prototypes, slides, and one-pagers by talking to Claude
- Announced alongside Opus 4.7 and explicitly tied to that model's visual capability
- Available in research preview on Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise plans
- Part of a broader Anthropic push toward artifact-first workflows rather than chat-only output

## Why it matters

Claude Design makes a product-level bet that many high-value agent outputs should be artifacts users can refine and share, not just answers in a conversation. That pushes Anthropic closer to design, presentation, and internal-report workflows that usually live in separate tools.

## Caveats

- Research preview means product shape and reliability are still moving
- Output quality likely depends heavily on prompt specificity, especially given early reports that Opus 4.7 is more literal than 4.6
