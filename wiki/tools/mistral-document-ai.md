# Mistral Document AI

Mistral Document AI is a document-intelligence product for extracting and structuring information from documents. It belongs in document intelligence because the main value is handling PDFs, forms, and enterprise document flows rather than general chat.

## Current status (as of 2026-04-22)

- Positioned for document understanding and information extraction
- Relevant across finance, healthcare, and general operations where structured data must be pulled out of messy files
- Good example of document AI maturing into a dedicated product surface
