# HeyGen

HeyGen is an avatar-video platform for synthetic presenters and reusable digital personas. It belongs in the avatar-video category because the product is organized around generated people and presenter workflows, not just generic video generation.

## Current status (as of 2026-04-22)

- Strong avatar-first product positioning
- Relevant for marketing, education, training, and enterprise communication workflows
