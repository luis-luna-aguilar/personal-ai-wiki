# FutureHouse

FutureHouse is a science-agent platform aimed at research and discovery workflows. It belongs in the science-agent-platform layer: a product surface built around agentic scientific work rather than a general-purpose assistant with occasional science demos.

## Current status (as of 2026-04-22)

- Positioned around AI systems for scientific research and discovery
- Product story centers on research workflows rather than generic chat
- Good fit for the growing cluster of agentic systems aimed at literature-heavy scientific work

## Why it matters

- Science is becoming one of the clearest verticals where agentic systems may compound advantage
- FutureHouse is one of the clearest named products in that category from the workbook
