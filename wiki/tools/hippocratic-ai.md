# Hippocratic AI

Hippocratic AI is a healthcare-focused agent company centered on safety-constrained conversational systems for medical workflows. The key distinction is domain-specific training and healthcare-oriented operational design rather than broad assistant positioning.

## Current status (as of 2026-04-22)

- Positioned explicitly around healthcare use cases
- Safety framing is central to the product story
- Better read as a vertical healthcare-agent company than as a general voice AI vendor
