# Gemini

Google's AI chatbot and assistant product, built on the Gemini model family. Combines conversational AI with search, productivity features, and integration into the Google ecosystem.

## Current status (as of 2026-04-10)

- Custom interactive visualizations: answers questions with charts and visuals directly in chat (ask Gemini Pro to "show me" or "help me visualize")
- Notebooks: dedicated workspaces for grouping chats, uploading files, and adding persistent instructions

## Strengths

- Deep Google ecosystem integration (Workspace, Search, Android)
- Multi-modal capabilities across text, image, and code

## Weaknesses / caveats

- Thin source coverage in this wiki so far (newsletter blurb only)
- Feature parity vs. ChatGPT and Claude not yet mapped

## Recent changes

- [2026-04-10] Custom visualizations and notebooks features announced; rolling out to paid accounts
