# Zo

Zo is Zocdoc's AI phone assistant for patient-access workflows. It fits healthcare AI because the product is built around healthcare appointment and scheduling operations rather than generic call handling.

## Current status (as of 2026-04-22)

- Focused on patient scheduling and phone-based workflow automation
- Represents the growing operational side of healthcare AI, not only clinical decision support
