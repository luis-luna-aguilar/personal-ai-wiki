# Tempus

Tempus is an AI-enabled healthcare and precision-medicine platform. In this wiki it belongs under healthcare AI because the product stack combines clinical data infrastructure with physician-facing AI workflow surfaces.

## Current status (as of 2026-04-22)

- Broader platform than a single chatbot-style product
- Strong healthcare data and workflow orientation
- More infrastructure-heavy than many AI-native healthcare startups
