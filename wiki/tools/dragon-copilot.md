# Dragon Copilot

Dragon Copilot is Microsoft's unified clinical voice assistant for healthcare. It belongs in healthcare AI because the product is purpose-built around clinical documentation, information retrieval, and workflow automation rather than generic enterprise assistance.

## Current status (as of 2026-04-22)

- Built around clinician-facing voice and documentation workflows
- Strong signal that healthcare-specific AI products are consolidating around operational assistants, not only ambient transcription
- The workbook's separate nursing note should be treated as expansion of the same product, not a distinct page
